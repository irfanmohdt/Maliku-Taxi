About Us

MALIKU TAXI is a 24-hour taxi service dedicated to providing reliable and efficient transportation for all passengers. Our mission is to cater to the diverse needs of travelers, whether they are residents, tourists, or business professionals. With a fleet of well-maintained vehicles, including Eeco and Ertiga cars as well as an auto-rickshaw, we ensure that our customers have a comfortable and enjoyable journey, regardless of their destination.

At MALIKU TAXI, we pride ourselves on our commitment to customer satisfaction. Our drivers are highly trained, friendly, and knowledgeable about the local area, ensuring a safe and pleasant experience for every ride. We understand the importance of convenience, which is why our services are available 24/7, making it easy for anyone to book a ride at any time.

Whether you're planning a sightseeing tour around Kerala, heading to the airport, or simply need a reliable ride to your destination, MALIKU TAXI is here for you. Our open-door policy welcomes everyone, and we are dedicated to providing excellent service to all our passengers, making every journey memorable. Choose MALIKU TAXI for your transportation needs and experience the difference!
